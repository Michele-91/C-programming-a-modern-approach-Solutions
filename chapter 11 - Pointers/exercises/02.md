## Question: 
<pre>
  If i is an int variable and p and q are pointers to int, which of the following
  assignments are legal?

  (a) p = i;      (d) p = &q;     (g) p = *q;
  (b) *p = &i;    (e) p = *&q;    (h) *p = q;
  (c) &p = q;     (f) p = q;      (i) *p = *q;
</pre>
## Answer:
<pre>
  (e), (f), and (i)
</pre>
